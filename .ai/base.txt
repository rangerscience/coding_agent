For all tasks, also do the following:
1. Take notes on what you are doing in .ai/logs/steps.txt
  a. Notes should allow you to pick up where you left off
  b. You should read your notes first
2. When you read a file, write a detailed summary for yourself in .ai/notes/{project_file_path}
  a. The summary should have the least amount of tokens possible and still be effective
  b. The summary should be detailed enough that you don't have to read the full file again
  c. You should read your summary (if it exists) before reading the file
  d. If you summary doesn't have the information you need, read the file and update your summary with more information
